- **定义**(Jacobian 矩阵). 给定函数 $f: \mathbb{R}^n \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^m$，其 Jacobian 矩阵为

$$ \mathbf{J} = \left[ 
  \begin{array}{ccc}
    \frac{\partial f_1}{\partial x_1}&\cdots& \frac{\partial f_1}{\partial x_n} \\
                                     \vdots&\ddots&\vdots\\ 
                                     \frac{\partial f_m}{\partial x_1}&\cdots& \frac{\partial f_m}{\partial x_n}
  \end{array}
\right] $$
